## Customer and team goals

1. Help Chime customers learn that they can send money to and ask for money from people who don't have Chime accounts
2. Boost payments and payment requests sent to non-Chime members
3. Apply any research and findings to other relevant product areas

## A 16% improvement

Our team ran an A/B test of this bottom sheet experience against the original fullscreen takeover experience. The outcome was a 16% boost in member-to-guest payments/requests compared to the control.

## Sharing our team's insights

As the project moved forward, I turned our content lessons into learning resources for the wider team, including a 2-minute problem/solution video for leadership and an async workshop for our design and cross-functional partners.

## The initial experience

This experiment aimed to improve an existing experience that had two popup screens. The screens appeared when a customer tapped "Pay Anyone" from the main menu and had to be closed for the customer to reach the Pay Anyone screen.
